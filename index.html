Your Experience Step by Step

  1. Book Your Session

    Choose a date and time that suits you and get ready for an unforgettable creative adventure.

  2. Arrive & Get a Welcome Drink

    Step into our vibrant space, relax, and get in the mood for some messy fun.

  3. Gear Up

    Put on a protective suit, gloves, and shoe covers—so you can go all in without worrying about the mess.

  4. Choose Your Colors

    Pick your favourite paints and get ready to unleash your creativity. No rules, just play!

  5. Create & Have Fun

    Splash, pour, beat, or spin your way to an abstract masterpiece.

  6. Pick Up Your Artwork

    Your painting will be ready for pickup in 24-48 hours once it dries.

Painting Techniques

Discover different ways to create your abstract masterpiece. No skills needed, just pure creative fun!

Pouring

Pour different colors and watch them blend into mesmerizing patterns.

Spinning

Create radial designs by spinning your canvas after applying paint.

Boxing

Punch your way to artistic expression with paint-covered boxing gloves.

Balloons

Create playful splatter patterns by popping paint-filled balloons on canvas.
